WEDDING FAQ

When should I RSVP by?

We kindly ask that you RSVP by March 15th.

What is the attire?

Formal – ladies in long gowns or cocktail dresses; gentlemen in tuxes or dark suits, but ties are optional. Please note that the ceremony will take place on a lawn. Block heels or wedge shoes are recommended. The cocktail hour will be indoor/outdoor and dinner and dancing will take place indoors. We recommend bringing a warm layer for the outdoor portion of the program.

Are children welcome?

Our venue has limited space, if your child is not listed on your invitation, we kindly ask that you arrange for a babysitter for the night.

Do you have a gift registry?

We kindly request no gifts. Your presence is the only gift we need, along with cool dance moves.

What time should I arrive to the ceremony?

Guests can arrive around 3:00-3:15pm. The ceremony will begin promptly at 3:30pm.

Is there parking at the venue?

Yes, there is a large parking lot at Kohl Mansion with plenty of parking.

Will transportation be provided?

Shuttles will be provided for the wedding party and family staying at Rosewood Sand Hill. There will be no other transportation provided.

Will there be meal options for guests with dietary restrictions?

Yes, definitely! Please let us know your specific dietary needs when you RSVP.

I’m having trouble deciding on an entrée. Which should I choose?

We have a fabulous caterer with a spring inspired menu, and you can’t go wrong with any of the options! Andrew’s choice is the fish, very delicious and not dry.
